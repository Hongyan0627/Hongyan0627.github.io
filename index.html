About Me

My name is Yi Lu (卢祎 in Chinese).

I'm a second year M.Phil. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My advisor is Prof. James Cheng.

Before joining the Chinese University of Hong Kong, I got my bachelor's degree in School of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, China.

I am going to pursue my Ph.D. in Massachusetts Institute of Technology in this fall.
